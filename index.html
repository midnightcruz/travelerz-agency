More Info on the Statue of Liberty

What you can see at the Statue of Liberty...

  • The Statue of Liberty - features a vast collection of photographs, prints and artifacts that tell the story of the monument throughout history.
  • The Visitor Information Station - features brochures, maps and memorabilia, and also gives visitors a chance to watch a short documentary about the making of the Statue of Liberty.
  • Audio Tour of Liberty Island - If you don't want to take a self-guided tour of Liberty Island, take an audio tour that will take your around the key attractions and sites on Liberty Island, and also walk you through the monument.
  • The Torch Exhibit - The display features a collection of diagrams, drawings, cartoons, renderings and photographs of the torch over the course of the monument's history.
